💊 Common Medicines

🤕 Pain & Fever

Paracetamol 500mg every 4-6 hours
Ibuprofen 200-400mg every 6-8 hours
Aspirin 300-600mg every 4-6 hours (adults)

🤧 Cold & Allergies

Cetirizine (Zyrtec) 10mg once daily
Loratadine (Claritin) 10mg once daily
Pseudoephedrine (Sudafed) 60mg every 4-6 hours

🤢 Stomach Issues

Loperamide (Imodium) 2mg after each loose stool
Pepto-Bismol 30ml every 30 min (max 8 doses)
Antacids (Tums) 1-2 tablets as needed

😴 Sleep & Relaxation

Melatonin 1-3mg before bed
Diphenhydramine (Benadryl) 25-50mg at bedtime

🩹 Topical Treatments

Bengay / Icy Hot Apply to affected area 3-4 times daily
Hydrocortisone Cream Apply thin layer 2-4 times daily
⚠️ Always read package instructions & consult a doctor if symptoms persist
